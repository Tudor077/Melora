Melora

Powered by your Spotify taste

Find your next favorite song. Every single day.

Melora pulls fresh tracks from the artists and genres you already love. Preview them, swipe through on your phone, and save the keepers to Spotify in one tap.

Hourly & daily picks

A fresh batch of songs on a schedule, built from your top artists and the genres you actually listen to.

Swipe to discover

On your phone, every track is full-screen. Swipe to the next one and it plays automatically. Like Reels, but for new music.

BPM at a glance

Every song shows its tempo, with quick filters for slow, mid and fast, so you find the energy you're after.

Save what you love

Tap the heart to add a track to your Liked Songs, or export a whole session as a Spotify playlist in one tap.

🪟 Installing on Windows

Run the .msi and follow the wizard. Windows SmartScreen may show a notice for new publishers, so choose More info, Run anyway. Published by Melora.

🤖 Installing on Android

Open the .apk and allow installs from your browser when prompted (Settings → Install unknown apps). Updates keep your login.
